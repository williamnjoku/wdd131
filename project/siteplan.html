HoopHub - Site Plan

Site Name

HoopHub

This name clearly communicates the purpose of the website – to serve as a central resource for learning about basketball, including its history, rules, and cultural significance.

Site Purpose

This website is an educational platform designed to teach visitors about the fundamentals of basketball. It covers the origins of the game, official rules, iconic moments, influential players, and its global cultural impact.

Scenarios

  • Who invented basketball, and how has the game evolved over time?
  • What are the official rules of basketball, including violations and player positions?

Color Schema

  • Orange (#f4511e): Used for headers, nav, and backgrounds.
  • Navy Blue (#0a0f3d): Used for accents like buttons and borders.
  • Beige (#fdf6f0): Used for body background to ensure readability.

Typography

  • Roboto(Bold) – For headers and navigation items.
  • Roboto – For body content and paragraphs.
